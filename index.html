This comprehensive guide was created as part of the Holberton School hackathon 2025. Our team, ATM Corp., has compiled the most essential information about Large Language Models to help students and developers understand this revolutionary technology.

Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced developer looking to expand your knowledge, this guide provides practical insights and technical details to help you navigate the world of LLMs.
